Built By An Applicant

Why I Created GradOS

A tool built from real application struggles, not from a business plan.

The Story

While applying for Master's and PhD opportunities, I found myself managing dozens of universities, professors, deadlines, recommendation letters, research projects and scholarship opportunities. Everything was scattered across spreadsheets, documents, email threads and notebooks. It quickly became difficult to keep track of what had been completed and what still needed attention.



I searched for a tool specifically designed for graduate applicants but couldn't find one that combined applications, professor tracking, referee management, deadlines and opportunity discovery in a single place. So I decided to build GradOS.

The Mission

Organization

Help applicants stay organized throughout their graduate journey.

Efficiency

Reduce time spent managing spreadsheets and scattered notes.

Opportunity Discovery

Make it easier to find and track opportunities.
